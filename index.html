ABOUT THE HEC GROUP

HEC Group of Institutions ranks amongst the top educational hubs in the Uttarakhand region. Established in 2002, HEC aims at bridging the gap between learning and leading. Ever since its conception, the college offers students the widest possible scope for academic exploration and 360-degree development in employability skills. During the course of its two decades of existence, the college has continuously risen towards excellence, growth and recognition..

It is affiliated with HNB Garhwal University, Srinagar, Garhwal and Sri Dev Suman Uttarakhand University, Badshahithaul, Tehri Garhwal. The College offers various Graduate, Post Graduate, PG Diploma and Certificate courses. These educational programs are in the various fields of Commerce, Management, Science, Arts, Computer Science, Lib. Science, Hotel Management and Yoga Ability improvement. HEC incorporates a solid combination of knowledge, research and industrial exposure as a part of its curriculum. In addition to academic opportunities, the Institution also aims at co-curricular activities and events, wherein each student is offered a platform to display his or her varied skill sets as well as dormant talents. Since its establishment in the year 2002, HEC Group of Institutions has grown into one of the most reputed organizations.
